What We Do

Residential & Light Commercial Electrical

From a flickering light to a full home rewire, we handle the work right the first time — with permits pulled and code followed.

Panel Upgrades

Upgrade aging 100-amp panels to 200-amp service. Safer, more capacity, and ready for modern appliances and EVs.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 home charging stations installed for Tesla, Ford, Rivian, and any J1772-compatible EV. Permit included.

Whole-Home Rewiring

Replace outdated knob-and-tube or aluminum wiring throughout your home. We minimize drywall damage and clean up after.

Lighting Design & Install

Recessed cans, under-cabinet, landscape, and chandeliers. Better light makes a real difference in how your home feels.

Emergency Repairs

Lost power, sparks, burning smells, or tripping breakers? We answer the phone 24/7 and dispatch fast across the valley.

Generator Installation

Whole-home standby generators from Generac, Kohler, and Briggs & Stratton. Don't lose power when the storm hits.

Smart Home Wiring

Structured wiring, smart switches, hardwired cameras, networked thermostats — done right behind the walls.
